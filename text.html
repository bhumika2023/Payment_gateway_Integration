"Giving is not just about making a donation,its about making a difference"

Not all of us can do great things.But we can do small things with great love. 1 in 11 children go to work when they should be in school. Donate for child education in India, support them with fees, school supplies, footwear, transport and opportunities to showcase their talents. A little help of yours can prevent the situation getting more worse for them. Even a small donation would help us a lot. To help the poor to raise their head and face the future with pride and hope with The Sparks Foundation.
